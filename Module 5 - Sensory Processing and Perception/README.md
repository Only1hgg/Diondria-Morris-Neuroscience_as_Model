**Module 05 — Sensory Processing and Perception**

The brain receives electrical spikes. Everything between raw physical energy and conscious experience is computation, and Module 05 mapped that computation across every sensory modality. The visual system processes through four stages before object identity emerges: V1 extracts oriented edges, V2 integrates them into contours, V4 processes shape and color, and inferotemporal cortex recognizes whole objects and faces. The brain runs two parallel visual streams simultaneously: the ventral "what" pathway for object identity and the dorsal "where/how" pathway for spatial processing. Most computer vision systems implement only the ventral stream. Robotics requires both. Across hearing, touch, taste, and smell, the brain reuses the same organizational logic, retinotopy for vision, tonotopy for hearing, and somatotopy for touch. Applying a proven architectural template instead of reinventing structure for each sense. Olfaction is the only sense that bypasses the thalamus entirely, projecting directly to the amygdala and hippocampus, which is the mechanistic reason smells trigger vivid emotional memories in a way other stimuli rarely match.

The lab implemented the retinal stage of visual processing from scratch. A Difference of Gaussians filter was built to model the center-surround receptive fields of retinal ganglion cells. A narrow excitatory Gaussian minus a wider inhibitory Gaussian producing the Mexican hat profile that responds to local contrast rather than absolute brightness. That filter was applied to three test images covering oriented edges, circular features, and a synthetic natural scene, making the first stage of the visual hierarchy tangible rather than theoretical. Gabor filters were also implemented as models of V1 simple cells, orientation-selective and spatially localized, directly analogous to the learned filters that emerge in the first convolutional layer of a trained CNN. The convergence between what biology evolved and what gradient descent independently discovers is one of the strongest validation points for brain inspired AI design, and this lab built enough technical grounding to understand both why that convergence happens and exactly where it ends.
